What is Pop-It! exactly?

It's a child's game that can be compared to the process of compressing and bursting of a bubble wrap. Our products provide the stress relief which can be chieved by popping the bubbles. But unlike film, Pop It can be pressed, with the same characteristic sensation, endlessly. Dozens of silicone bubbles are simply squizzed out in the other direction.

Choose your Pop-It!

Rules of the game

Players take turns and click on the bumps. It is imperative to finish the row that you started. You can press a maximum of 3 bubbles at the same time (they must be near, you cannot press several pimples at once, if they are not close to each other) The task is to make the opponent go last. Who went last - he lost!)
